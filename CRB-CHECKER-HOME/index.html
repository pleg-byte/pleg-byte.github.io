Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our help center! Here you can find answers to questions frequently asked by our users.

How can I check my CRB status?

Registering to check your CRB status is free of charge. First, download our app. Create your account with an email address that is the same as the address that you use for your financial activities. This step is important! Fill in your profile with as much information as possible to receive accurate credit status updates. Additionally, you will receive updates more often if your profile has more information.

What if I forget my password?

You can easily reset your password by following these steps:

  • Go to the login page
  • Click “Forgot my password” underneath the login area
  • Type in the email address you used for registering with the CRB Status Checker
  • We will immediately send you an email containing a link for resetting your password
  • Check your inbox
  • Click the link in the email
  • Set a new password

Why am I not receiving any status updates?

The number of updates you receive depends on the country that you live in. Participants in certain countries receive more updates than those in some other locations. Also, participants are selected according to age, gender, financial history, level of education, etc. All these requirements are set by financial institutions that provide the data. We suggest that you fill in your profile with as much information as possible to receive the most accurate updates and receive them more frequently.

Why am I not qualified for a status update?

For some updates, a target group is set in advance based on certain characteristics that are not included in your profile with us. This is why it can occur that you are not part of a specific update’s target group.

How can I withdraw my money?

Status checks need to be completed in order to get a reward. The payout threshold depends on the selected payment method. When you reach the payment threshold, you can transfer the money using the payment method you choose. Note that the payment method must be linked to the same email address you’re using for completing status checks. You can check the payment methods available for your country on your profile under the "Rewards" section.
